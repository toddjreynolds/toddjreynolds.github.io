Frequently Asked Questions

Why is First Verse only for the Book of Mormon?

It won't be for long. There is an update planned which will include all standard works of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

Why can't I select text in First Verse?

I noticed that kids often read and track with their fingers. Doing so on a device meant a lot of unintended text selection that was a big distraction. I might include a setting in the future that allows text selection to be enabled. Maybe.
